How To Play

You're the President with a Twitter addiction and a love for tariffs!

Goal: Survive 4 years without getting impeached while making yourself rich.

Controls:

  • Click the TWEET! button to issue random policy tweets
  • Each tweet affects the market and may trigger scandals
  • Use your Crony Cards strategically to maximize profits
  • Keep an eye on the Scandal Meter - if it fills up, you're impeached!
  • Watch the Market Health - if it crashes completely, you lose support

Win by: Reaching the end of your 4-year term with maximum wealth

Lose by: Getting impeached or completely crashing the economy
